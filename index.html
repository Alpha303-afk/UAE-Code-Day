UAE Code Day

UAE Codes

UAE Codes is an annual event that forms a crucial part of the National Program for Coders, an initiative spearheaded by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai.

Falling on the 29th of October every year, “UAE Codes” celebrates the nation’s transformation to a digital economy.

Main goal of the UAE Code Day


The main goal of the UAE Code Day is to celebrate and promote coding across the UAE by providing resources, event details, and inspiration to encourage participation in coding activities. The webpage aims to raise awareness about the importance of coding for the UAE's future and showcase opportunities for learning and engagement in programming.

Audience

The target audience includes students, teachers, coding enthusiasts, and anyone interested in technology and programming within the UAE. It is especially geared toward young learners and educators who want to explore coding and its potential impact on future career opportunities and digital innovation.

Drives Innovation and Economic Growth:


Coding skills are fundamental for building software, apps, and digital platforms, which play a crucial role in supporting the UAE’s growing tech industry and diversifying its economy.
